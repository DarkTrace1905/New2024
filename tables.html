Tables in HTML

Tables are used to display data in a structured format. Here is an example of a simple table:

Name Age City
Alice 30 New York
Bob 25 Los Angeles
Charlie 35 Chicago

You can customize the appearance of the table using CSS. For example, you can change the background color, font size, and border style.
